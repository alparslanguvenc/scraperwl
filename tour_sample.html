What you can expect

There's no doubt that Bangkok is beautiful, and the best way to discover its rich history and culture is on a bike and longtail boat! Come with me on a one of a kind private tour where you'll move around the city like local.

See Bangkok from a real local perspective while cycling through the city. Cycle pass iconic landmarks like the memorial bridge and a 200 years old white mosque. And how about an oldie but goodie? Bangkok's Old Town is the place to go. Visit the fascinating temple Wat Intraratharam where the ashes of King Taksin are held in a stupa.

It's lunchtime! Grab a delicious lunch by the railway track and pass by a local market along the way. Once you're done enjoying typical Thai delicacies, I'll take you to the royal Wat Paknam on the side of the Chao Phraya River.

Before heading back, take a break at a unique artist house. This creative hub has great coffee and traditional Thai puppetry shows. And for a grand finale, let's make our way back by longtail boat. Relax and enjoy the scenic ride and the sights from the water.
